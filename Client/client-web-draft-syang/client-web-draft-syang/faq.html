FAQ

Commonly Asked Questions

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We love making custom orders! Please email us at luxurypressbynie@gmail.com with a subject line containing "Custom Order Inquiry".

How long does the nails usually last?

Follow my directions on how to nail prep.
If you're using nail glue, it shoudl last from 1-2 weeks.
If you're using nail stickers, it may last 1-3 days.

Are the nails/nail sets reusable?

Yes all nail sets are reusable, if you follow my directions on nail removal.

How do I know my nail size?

We have sizing kits you can order for $5.00, this is the MOST accurate way to size your nails. If you decide not to purchase one, you can use soft tape measure to measure your nails and convert it over to our sizing chart.
